This is how you get your custom made piece

  • Simon will sketch a design for your garment.
  • Within three days, you will receive the sketch and a price quote for your piece.
  • Throughout the process, you can chat with Simon to ensure everything is exactly to your liking.
  • The sketch costs 39 euro, but this money will be deducted from the total price when you place your order.
  • The total price will be determined between you and Simon. Shipping and import costs are additional and vary by country.
