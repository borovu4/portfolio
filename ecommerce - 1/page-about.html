ABOUT US

UNIQUE

It’s all about you. That is what we believe. What is it that you want to wear? How would you like to emphasize your personality or mood? How would you like to distinguish yourself and stand out from the crowd? If you look good, you feel good! We are all unique and what better way to show your uniqueness to the world by wearing an outfit thats like no others but only like you!

PERSONAL DREAM WARDROBE

We can help you in realizing your personal dream wardrobe. You don’t need to go to a store anymore to see and buy what others have made for everyone. Why choose from existing fashion when you can create your own.

REVOLUTIONARY IN FASHION

This is what we believe. We want to make a 180 degrees turn and let demand determine what is made instead of letting you choose from supply. Instead of push we believe in pull because you are a unique individual. You are completely in control. We revolutionize the fashion industry. We have designers from all over the world with the best qualifications and experience ready to make a unique piece for you. Just one piece thats only for you and nobody else.

ONLINE ECONOMY

Nowadays we find ourselves in an online economy. We order everything online and because of this we gained a certain freedom of choice. The offer and availability has suddenly become way bigger. The world is at your feet. This allows you to make a choice of what you buy, how you look and what you wear.

SLOW FASHION

Why look the same, if you are not. With unique clothes you feel better and more confident. You feel proud and contribute to a better world. Forget fast fashion. Waste is nonsense. Everybody has the chance to feel and look good, with long lasting materials and where every designer gets a chance to be noticed. We all contribute to a better world and so do you!

ENVIRONMENT

Why wear cheap clothes that everybody wears. Independently of looking the same you don't contribute to a better world. Fast and cheap fashion is offered but somebody has to pay the price. If you don't pay the price in the store somebody who is making it or the environment pays for it. There is no such thing as cheap fashion.

SUPPORT YOUR LOCAL

There are many fashion designers, established or just out of university who have great design abilities. Not only are they qualified to design for you, they are very willing. Whether if you're fond of European design and want to look unique and show off your good taste, there are probably a lot of designers in your area that are very talented, whom you might never heard of. Trough our system you are able to get in contact with all talent. Abroad and Nearby!

ITSJU

We gave this a lot of thought. We put a lot of energy, blood sweat and tears in making uniqueness possible for everybody. For the ones who feels and craves it and the ones whom creates it. Its all about you! Lets get started!
